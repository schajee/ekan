EKAN (Easy Knowledge Archive Network) is a comprehensive open data portal designed to make government and public data accessible, discoverable, and usable for everyone. Our platform serves as a central hub for datasets, resources, and information that drive transparency, innovation, and informed decision-making.

Our Mission

We believe that open data is a cornerstone of democratic governance and societal progress. EKAN facilitates the sharing of high-quality datasets across government departments, research institutions, and organizations, enabling citizens, researchers, and businesses to access the information they need to create positive impact.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Datasets

Access thousands of datasets across multiple categories including health, education, environment, transportation, and more.

Advanced Search

Find exactly what you need with our powerful search and filtering capabilities across datasets, organizations, and topics.

Multiple Formats

Download data in various formats including CSV, JSON, XML, PDF, and Excel to suit your analytical needs.

Quality Assured

All datasets undergo quality verification and are maintained by trusted government departments and organizations.

Open Data Standards

EKAN follows international open data standards and best practices, ensuring our platform remains interoperable, accessible, and sustainable. We support various open licenses that allow for reuse, redistribution, and commercial use while maintaining proper attribution.

For Developers

Access our data programmatically through our comprehensive REST API. Whether you're building applications, conducting research, or creating visualizations, our API provides reliable access to all published datasets with proper authentication and rate limiting.
