    The terms of use for datasets of the federal authorities on openasterisk.net are determined by the existing legal framework. If there is a statutory requirement to cite the source, this is indicated by the appropriate symbol next to the dataset concerned. In all other cases, users are recommended to cite the source.

    The symbols next to each dataset indicate what use is permitted. All data owners use the same symbols.

    Public Domain

    • You may use this dataset for non-commercial purposes.
    • You may use this dataset for commercial purposes.
    • You are recommended to provide the source (author, title and link to the dataset).

    Attribution

    • You may use this dataset for non-commercial purposes.
    • You may use this dataset for commercial purposes.
    • You must provide the source (author, title and link to the dataset).

    Non Commerical

    • You may use this dataset for non-commercial purposes.
    • You may use this dataset for commercial purposes, but you must seek prior permission from the data owner.
    • You are recommended to provide the source (author, title and link to the dataset).

    Open Asterisk provides a usage model for other Pakistani authorities and organisations with a mandate from the state.
